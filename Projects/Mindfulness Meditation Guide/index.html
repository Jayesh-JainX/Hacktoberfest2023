Mindfulness Meditation Guide

Guided Meditation Exercises

Exercise 1: Deep Breathing

Close your eyes and take a deep breath in for 4 seconds. Hold your breath for 4 seconds, and then exhale for 4 seconds. Repeat this for 5 minutes.

Exercise 2: Body Scan

Lie down, close your eyes, and focus on each part of your body, starting from your toes and moving up. Release tension as you go.

Listen to the calming sounds of nature for relaxation. Click here to begin.
